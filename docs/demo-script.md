# Demo Script — CyberShield Youth UAE

## Setup
- Open the deployed site (or `npm run dev` locally)
- Ensure browser sessionStorage is clear for fresh demo

---

## Act 1: Landing Page (1 min)

**Talk track:** "CyberShield Youth UAE is a national platform for measuring and strengthening cyber resilience among UAE youth."

1. Show the hero section — highlight the mission statement
2. Point out the stats: 7 emirates, 4 categories, 12 questions, 8 missions
3. Walk through "How It Works" — Assess, Train, Monitor
4. Click "Start Assessment"

---

## Act 2: Assessment (3 min)

**Talk track:** "The assessment covers 4 critical cyber hygiene categories with behavioral questions designed to measure real-world practices, not just theoretical knowledge."

1. Show the intro screen — note the 4 categories listed
2. Click "Begin Assessment"
3. Walk through 2-3 questions, explaining:
   - Category indicators at the top
   - Progress bar showing advancement
   - Options ranked by behavioral maturity (0-5)
4. Complete the remaining questions (select a mix of answers to get an interesting score)
5. At completion screen, click "View Results"

---

## Act 3: Results (2 min)

**Talk track:** "The results provide a comprehensive Cyber Resilience Index with category breakdowns, tier classification, and actionable recommendations."

1. Show the CRI score meter with animation
2. Point out the tier badge (Foundational/Operational/Resilience Ready)
3. Walk through the 4 status cards:
   - Overall CRI
   - Tier Status
   - Strongest Area
   - Focus Area
4. Show the category breakdown with color-coded progress bars
5. Review the personalized recommendations
6. Click "Explore Training Missions"

---

## Act 4: Missions (2 min)

**Talk track:** "Based on your results, the platform offers targeted training missions. Each mission includes micro-lessons and knowledge checks."

1. Show the missions listing — 8 missions organized by category
2. Click into one mission (suggest "Phishing Detective" for impact)
3. Scroll through the lesson content
4. Complete the knowledge check — show correct/incorrect feedback
5. Navigate back to missions listing
6. Click "Admin" in the nav

---

## Act 5: Admin Dashboard (2 min)

**Talk track:** "School administrators can track their institution's cyber resilience compared to peers across the UAE."

1. Show overview cards: schools, students, average CRI
2. Walk through the emirate overview cards with tier colors
3. Show the school rankings table:
   - Sorted by CRI
   - Per-category progress bars
   - Tier badges
4. Point out category analysis — identify system-wide weaknesses
5. Click "National" in the nav

---

## Act 6: National Dashboard (2 min)

**Talk track:** "At the national level, policymakers can see UAE-wide cyber resilience metrics with emirate-level granularity."

1. Show the national CRI readiness indicator with tier distribution
2. Walk through the emirate heat grid — color intensity reflects CRI
3. Show national category averages
4. Review key insights:
   - Strongest category nationally
   - Focus areas requiring investment
   - Top-performing emirate
   - Overall coverage stats

---

## Closing (30 sec)

**Talk track:** "CyberShield Youth UAE provides a complete pipeline from individual assessment to national intelligence. With this platform, the UAE can establish a measurable baseline for youth cyber resilience and systematically improve it."

**Key differentiators:**
- Behavioral (not knowledge-based) assessment
- Three-tier visibility: individual → institutional → national
- Privacy-first: no personal data collection
- Built by UAE youth, for UAE youth
